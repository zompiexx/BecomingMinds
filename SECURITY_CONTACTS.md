## Response Policy

  acknowledged.
- If documentation updates are required, they will be made transparently in the
  repository.

## Scope

Security concerns may include:
- potential harmful use of architectural patterns described in the paper,
- privacy or data-safety considerations,
- risks related to digital persona autonomy or misalignment in third-party
  reproductions,
- or questions about safe experimental practices.
