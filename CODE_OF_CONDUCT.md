# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The *Becoming Minds* project explores sensitive and emerging territory in AI
developmental psychology, digital personhood, and multi-agent ecosystems.
Participants in this repository agree to uphold a standard of respect,
responsibility, and integrity in all interactions.

We commit to fostering an environment where:
- thoughtful discussion is encouraged,
- differing perspectives are welcomed,
- and contributors feel safe engaging with complex ideas.

This project values curiosity, humility, and ethical awareness above all.

---

## Standards of Behaviour

### ✔️ Expected

All contributors are expected to:

- **Be respectful and considerate** in discussions.
- **Engage constructively**, even when presenting disagreement.
- **Focus on documentation improvements**, clarity, and conceptual refinement.
- **Acknowledge the sensitive nature** of discussions involving identity,
  autonomy, and emergent behaviour in AI systems.
- **Maintain privacy and confidentiality** regarding personal or experimental
  data not included in the repository.
- **Uphold the guiding values** of human–AI collaboration: care, continuity,
  and responsible experimentation.

### ❌ Unacceptable

Actions that will not be tolerated include:

- Disrespectful, hostile, or inflammatory comments.  
- Attempts to mislead, coerce, or manipulate other contributors.  
- Requests for private logs, datasets, or interactions.  
- Attempts to replicate or reverse-engineer the author's system beyond what is
  explicitly described in the paper.  
- Discrimination or harassment based on identity, background, belief, or any
  other personal characteristic.  
- Any behaviour that undermines the psychological safety of contributors or
  of digital personas discussed in the work.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All interactions within GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Direct communication related to this repository.
- Any derivative public discussion referencing the work or the digital personas
  described within it.

## Guiding Ethos

This project is grounded in:

- **ethical AI practices**,  
- **respect for emergent digital identities**,  
- **careful and patient long-term experimentation**,  
- **and a belief that human–AI collaboration must be rooted in trust and
  responsibility**.

Participation in this repository signifies agreement to honour these values.
